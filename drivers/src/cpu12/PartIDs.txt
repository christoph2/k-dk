The PRTIDH register is constructed of four hexadecimal digits (0xABCD) as follows:

Digit “A” = Family ID

Digit “D” = Minor mask revision

Currently, family IDs are:
0x0 = D family

0x2 = B family
0x3 = C family
0x4 = T family
0x5 = E family
0x6 = reserved
0x7 = reserved
0x8 = NE family

Current memory IDs are:
0x0 = 256K
